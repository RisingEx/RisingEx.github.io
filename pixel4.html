Pixel 4. Is it any good?

Pixel 4


16 October 2019

Is it any better than its predecessor?


Recently, Google has launched the newest addition to the Pixel lineup which is the Pixel 4 during its Made By Google event in New York City. For the past few months, there are so many leaks about the device and it sure has build up a lot of hype among people, including me.

The device come up with 2 variants, Pixel 4 and Pixel 4 XL. Both of them run Android 10 out of the box and have many unique features. They also come with three free month of Google One cloud storage for new members. Sadly, free unlimited original quality photo backups to Google Photos are not included.

Here is an impression video on Pixel 4 by Marques Brownlee

The Pixel 4 and Pixel 4 XL share the same specifications which is Qualcomm Snapdragon 855 SoC, 6 GB of RAM, 64or 128GB onboard storage with no microSD expansion, and no 3.5mm headphone jack.

One of the stunning new features in Pixel 4 devices is Night Sight with astrophotography. Originally, Night Sight comes with the launch of Pixel 3 devices which deliver outstanding performance of taking picture in low light condition. With the addition of astrophotography, no doubt that Night Sight mode has been improved and we can now see the beauty of the skies at night.

Motion Sense is a new feature to the Pixel line as it capable of sensing users' motion. On supported apps, you will able to utilize this function with waving your hand. You will be able to change a song, snooze alarm, and silence calls.
